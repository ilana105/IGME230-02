Running The Pass

This minigame will be made with pixels

The focus of the first mini-game will be running the pass.
The goal of the game is to perfectly read back all of the orders.

There will be a timer and if the player isn't done with the ticket then Ramsay will say something negative.If the user gets a perfect ticket, then Ramsay will say something positive.

The score will be shown at the end of the game, prompt you to enter your name and will record your score in the local storage.


Mechanics: Memorization, Matching

Control: Touch/Click

Instructions: Tap the dishes back in the correct order

At the start, the order will be shown on the screen for 3 seconds [will test].

Then the player will be shown the dishes and must pick the right ones.

In order to get a perfect score, there must be no mistakes and done within 10 seconds [will test as well].

There will be 2 difficulties, easy and hell mode.

For EASY mode:
-10 to 20 tickets
-Max 5 seconds to look at the tickets
-Max 12 seconds to complete the ticket

For HELL mode:
-25+ tickets
-Max 3 seconds to look at the tickets
-Max 8 seconds to complete the ticket

I will update the information once everything is finalized.

High Concept: Prove that you are worthy of the black jacket

Genre: Memory, Matching

Platform: Mobile friendly -- Phone, Tablet, PC
